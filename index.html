About BarrPeps

The BarrPeps database is a curated repository of antimicrobial peptides (AMPs) with demonstrated anticancer activity that are capable of crossing the blood-brain barrier (BBB). These peptides represent a promising class of therapeutics for the treatment of brain cancers, particularly glioblastoma and brain metastases.

Applications:

  • Drug discovery and development for brain cancers
  • Structure-activity relationship studies
  • Computational modeling and prediction
  • Comparative analysis of peptide therapeutics
